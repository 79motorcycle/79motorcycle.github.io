Meet The Team

Meet the face who is wanting to help others fully enjoy adventures in Tulum

Diego Calavante

CEO

Diego, originally from Brazil, moved to Tulum last year and quickly realized how challenging it was to get around without reliable transportation. Frustrated by the high costs and complicated rental processes offered by other companies, he decided to create Motorcycles 79. His goal was simple: to offer an affordable and reliable way for others to explore Tulum, empowering them to make the most of their adventures without the hassle

Frequently Asked Questions

To begin the rental process, simply contact us via WhatsApp with your desired rental duration and the number or type of scooters/ATVs you'd like to rent!

On the Fleet page, select the ATV or scooter you're interested in, and the daily rental price will be displayed. For longer rental durations, feel free to message us, and we’ll provide you with a special price.

Yes, you need to be 18 or older and hold a valid driver’s license to rent a scooter or ATV.

Once your booking is confirmed, we will deliver the scooter(s) and/or ATV(s) to the location you provide. Each rental comes with two helmets and a full tank of gas (please return with a full tank). When your rental duration ends, we will pick up the scooter(s) and/or ATV(s) from the agreed location.

We hope this never happens, but if you do have an accident, please contact us immediately. We’ll assist you and provide the necessary support to handle the situation in the best possible way. Your safety is our priority, so don’t hesitate to reach out.

Unlike many other rental services, we don’t require a security deposit. All we need is a simple copy of your passport to complete the booking and get you on the road!
